About Me!

As I stated on the index page, I am a first year, first semester student at SAIT, majoring in Graphic Design. I've lived in Calgary my entire life, born and raised in the same house I currently live in.

Pets

I have 2 cats, sisters, who are 8 years old. We got them for free from my barn, and one of them has been the most expensive cat we've ever owned.

In addition to my cats, I have a horse. She's, also, 8 years old. I bought her as a 6 year old in Saskatchewan. She's an off the track thoroughbred and raced for 2 years, earning about $11,000 dollars in her career.

Education

I've completed high school, with initial plans of becoming a vet tech. After experiencing some incidents in both my work and personal life, I decided that it wasn't the career path I wanted to take. Instead, I decided to put my creativity to good use and took the graphic design route. Don't regret that decision one bit.
